Pre-trip Checklist

Before embarking on your chapter

Once your trip is confirmed, we require a $200 deposit. A waiver and form will be sent to each guest of the trip. Please fill out all applicable information, and send it back to us. Your next steps are simple, dress according to the weather and show up at the location on the day of at the specified time.

Things to bring:

Other than a positive attitude and your basic winter clothing, we’ve got you covered. From fishing tackle, electronics, to hand warmers, spend your time worrying about having a good time!

  • Winter Jacket
  • Your Warmest Pair of Boots
  • Gloves
  • Snow Pants
  • Fishing License*

*If you have a fishing license, bring the PDF on your phone, or if you don’t have a license, we will can offer to help you buy your one day fishing license.
